# Fablewick — Hero Illustration Prompts (11 books)

**Style directive (prepend to every prompt):**
> Warm painterly children's book illustration, soft watercolor texture with gentle brushstrokes, cozy picture-book aesthetic, muted natural palette, slightly textured paper background, no text or lettering, 16:9 aspect ratio

## 1. The Brave Little Seed
A tiny brown seed sitting alone in dark, warm earth. Above the soil line, a sliver of bright sky with birds flying. A single green sprout is just beginning to push upward from the seed. A friendly worm watches nearby with a gentle smile. Palette: earthy browns, warm sage green, golden sunlight peeking through.

## 2. Pip and the Lost Cloud
A small field mouse standing on a grassy hilltop at golden hour, looking up at a single fluffy white cloud that has drifted too low, almost touching the treetops. The mouse reaches one tiny paw upward. Rolling green fields stretch behind them. Palette: cream sky, soft greens, warm gold, cotton-white cloud.

## 3. The Hundred-Year Stone
A round, friendly-looking gray stone sitting at the edge of a sparkling river. The stone has a subtle hopeful expression. Around it, small pebbles have already tumbled into the current. Morning mist rises from the water. Moss and tiny wildflowers have grown on the stone over the years. Palette: cool river blues, warm gray stone, misty amber morning light.

## 4. Moss and the Starry Night Sky
A small hedgehog (Moss) sitting on a mossy log at night, gazing up at a sky full of bright, swirling stars. The hedgehog's quills catch starlight. Fireflies float in the dark meadow around the log. A sense of wonder and quiet curiosity. Palette: deep midnight blue, warm coral-tinged stars, soft mossy greens.

## 5. The Quiet Bear
A large, gentle brown bear sitting very still beside a small wren bird in a forest clearing. The wren looks sad, head slightly tucked. The bear is simply present — not speaking, not reaching, just being there. Autumn leaves drift between them. Palette: warm sage greens, soft browns, muted amber leaves.

## 6. Tuli's Whistle
A red-orange fox sitting on a tree stump with cheeks puffed out comically, clearly trying very hard to whistle. Small music notes float around but are wobbly and crooked. Other woodland animals peek from behind trees, watching with encouraging expressions. Palette: warm amber, russet fox fur, forest greens, playful energy.

## 7. The Girl Who Stopped Looking
A young girl (Maya) mid-stroke in a swimming pool, viewed from slightly above. Crystal-clear blue water, lane dividers on each side. Her eyes are focused straight ahead — not looking sideways at the other lanes. Splash droplets catching light. Palette: pool blues, bright white splashes, warm skin tones, clean aqua.

## 8. The Boy Who Couldn't Walk
A young Franklin Roosevelt sitting at the edge of a wooden dock, legs dangling but still, looking out at a vast, shimmering ocean. His wheelchair is behind him. His expression is determined and thoughtful, not sad. Seagulls overhead, sailboats in the distance. Palette: ocean blues, warm wood tones, golden horizon.

## 9. The Stillest Man in the Room
An old man (J.P. Morgan) sitting very still at a long mahogany library table, surrounded by panicked men in suits rushing around him. Morgan is calm — hands folded, pocket watch on the table. Through tall library windows, a storm rages outside. He is the still center while everything moves. Palette: warm amber library light, dark mahogany, storm-gray windows, gold watch glint.

## 10. Hands in the Soil
A Gujarati grandfather (brown skin, receding hairline, weathered hands, simple cotton kurta) kneeling in a lush vegetable garden beside a small grandchild. Dada is gently pressing a seed into the dark soil with his thumb. Green tomato plants with small unripe fruit surround them. Warm late-afternoon golden light. Terracotta pots and a watering can nearby. Palette: rich sage greens, warm brown earth, golden afternoon light, terracotta.

## 11. Kitchen of Love
A Gujarati grandmother (Ba) standing at a kitchen counter, carefully cutting a ripe golden mango with a small knife. Warm kitchen light from a window. A plate of already-cut fruit sits on the counter — mangoes, bananas, pomegranate seeds. A small grandchild stands in the doorway watching, backlit. Ba doesn't know the child is there yet. The kitchen is warm, lived-in, full of love expressed through food. Palette: warm amber, kitchen yellows, mango gold, soft brown skin tones.
